## Vision

### 10x Check
The 10x version turns the chapter detail view into a living relationship dashboard. Not just a timeline of memories, but a surface that understands the relationship: when you last talked, how long you've known each other, a map of shared places, seasonal patterns, milestone markers, and ambient presence indicators. The timeline becomes context-aware, surfacing "on this day" memories and suggesting when to reach out based on decay patterns.

### Platonic Ideal
Opening a chapter feels like stepping into a private room that exists only for you and that person. The room remembers everything. Photos from 4 years ago look different from yesterday's through context (the app knows Barcelona was 4 years ago). Voice clips create a soundscape of the friendship. The room has "weather," warm when active, cooler when quiet. Nothing needs to be explained. You just feel it.

## Scope Decisions

| # | Proposal | Effort | Decision | Reasoning |
|---|----------|--------|----------|-----------|
| 1 | "On This Day" memories | S (~30 min CC) | ACCEPTED | Huge emotional payoff, near-zero effort. Re-engagement hook. |
| 2 | Relationship age counter | S (~5 min CC) | ACCEPTED | 5 lines of code. Pure delight. |
| 3 | Auto-play voice clip preview | M (~1 hr CC) | DEFERRED | High risk of annoyance. Needs real user feedback first. |
| 4 | Photo full-bleed detail view | S (~30 min CC) | ACCEPTED | Reuses daily card gradient pattern. Chapter feels incomplete without it. |
| 5 | Map dot visualization | M (~1 hr CC) | DEFERRED | Real scope (MapKit + clustering). Better with more location data. |
| 6 | Chapter color from photos | M (~45 min CC) | DEFERRED | Beautiful polish. Design decisions about tint subtlety. |
| 7 | Typing indicator | L (~2 hr CC) | DEFERRED | Requires WebSocket/polling infra that doesn't exist. |

## Accepted Scope (added to this plan)
- "On This Day" floating card when a memory from the same date in a prior year exists
- Relationship age counter in chapter header ("4 years, 2 months")
- Photo full-bleed detail view with daily card gradient pattern + swipe between photos

## Deferred to TODOS.md
- Auto-play voice clip preview on scroll (needs user validation)
- Map dot visualization (needs MapKit integration + real location data)
- Chapter color derived from photos (needs design polish pass)
- Typing indicator (needs real-time infrastructure)

## Core Design (from approved design doc)
- Bilateral conversation timeline (left = you, right = them)
- Variable-height photos with server-side aspect ratio
- Animated voice waveforms (accent color on playback)
- Styled text cards with optional emotion tags
- Rich metadata on every item (location, date)
- Fun voice recording with haptics + progress ring
- Fun text writing with warm sheet + optional emotion tags
- Scrapbook export deferred to v1.1 (repurposed from original Approach C)
